Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

TrafficAbuja is committed to the responsible use, storage, and safeguarding of your personal information. Our fundamental principles are:

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not share personal information with third parties for advertising, marketing, or unsolicited communications.
  • Personal information is not disclosed to external parties unless required by law.

Data Retention

We retain client data only for the necessary period which is service execution, record-keeping, and the refinement of our services. Data related to discontinued services is retained solely for analysis to improve service quality.

In accordance with your rights, you may:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information.
  • Request the anonymization of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for the use of your data for non-essential purposes.

Requests can be submitted via email and social platform. We will respond to all legitimate requests in a timely manner.
