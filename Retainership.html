Retainership Terms

A Retainership shall be established on mutually agreeable terms, which may be structured as a fixed amount, a percentage, or another defined framework. The specific terms shall reflect all relevant factors, including but not limited to:

  • The urgency and nature of the engagement,
  • The required timeline,
  • Available alternatives to achieve the stated outcomes,
  • The necessary level of collaboration.

This flexible approach ensures neither party is constrained by premature assumptions.

Important Note: All arrangements under this Retainership are distinct and separate from services catalogued under 'Purchased Inventory/Hired Network'.
